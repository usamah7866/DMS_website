About Our School

A legacy school shaping disciplined, capable, and confident learners.

Decent Model School has been serving parents since 1980 and continues to grow as a trusted educational institution in Gulshan Ravi, Lahore. Through its three campuses, the school supports children from early years to matric with a balanced focus on academics, values, growth, and opportunity.

Mission & Vision

A clear sense of purpose for learning, character, and long-term growth.

Mission

To provide quality education with discipline, care, and direction.

Decent Model School aims to provide meaningful education that helps students grow in knowledge, character, and confidence. The school is committed to structured teaching, responsible guidance, and an environment where every child can build a stronger future.

Vision

To prepare students for life with values, ability, and self-belief.

The school values its heritage while continuing to improve its methods, facilities, and student experience. It aims to help learners become thoughtful, capable, and morally aware individuals who are ready to take their place in society with confidence.

Background & History

Built through decades of service, trust, and community connection.

Established in 1980, Decent Model School began with a simple but lasting purpose: to offer accessible, disciplined, and values-based education to children in the community. Over the years, the school has grown into a multi-campus institution serving a wider range of learners and parents across Gulshan Ravi.

Today, the school serves students from nursery to matric and remains affiliated with B.I.S.E. Lahore. Its development reflects a long-term commitment to steady academic standards, practical student care, and a school culture rooted in discipline and opportunity.

  • Established in 1980
  • Affiliated with B.I.S.E. Lahore
  • Serving Nursery to Matric
  • Three active campuses in Gulshan Ravi
  • Focused on academic and character development
