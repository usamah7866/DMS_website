Academics at DMS

Learning that feels structured, modern, and easy to understand.

Academic Overview

From strong basics to board readiness.

The academic structure balances foundational learning, regular testing, classroom discipline, subject growth, co-curricular exposure, and digital skills across all stages.

Nursery to 10 Full school pathway
B.I.S.E. Board-linked direction

Levels of Education

Four clear stages help parents understand the academic journey.

Early Years

Nursery, KG, Prep

Focus on literacy, numeracy, speaking confidence, class routine, and early activity-based learning.

Primary

Class 1 to 5

Students build stronger subject understanding through reading, writing, mathematics, general knowledge, and regular class activities.

Middle and Secondary

Class 6 to 10

More advanced subject depth, exam readiness, discipline, and board-focused academic preparation become central.
